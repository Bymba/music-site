Footer

Those approaches have been tested with Firefox, Safari, Chrome, Opera, Internet Explorer 7 and higher.

When an HTML page contains a small amount of content, the footer can sometimes sit halfway up the page leaving a blank space underneath. This can look bad, particularly on a large screen. Web designers are often asked to push footers down to the bottom of the viewport. Below examples how this can be done.

  • Stick footer to bottom - position absolute technique
